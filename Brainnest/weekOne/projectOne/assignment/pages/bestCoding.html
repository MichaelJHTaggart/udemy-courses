Why I love to code

Ever since completing a bootcamp in 2021 I have been hungry for coding. I did a freelance website burlmountain, and was interviewing like crazy. I was still finishing my associates degree (a 2 year college degree), and was looking for a part-time job. I found a job at the bootcamp I just attended. Devmountain forced me to work with other young coders and solidify the basics while still finishing my degree. After a while I realized that each group that went through Devmountain made a Discord server to work together, and then it kind of fizzled out. So I decided that I wanted to create my own Discord server that I plan to maintain forever.

A place to work together

I wanted to create an environment of coders who could collaborate with other hungry coders who wanted to do cool stuff. Though I still haven't had the time to devote to a collab project yet on my server, I look forward to working with others to build fun projects!

I love to code, because it gives me the freedom to build whatever I want to build! I can build amazing tools that can be used all around the world right here from my computer. That is what is so amazing about learning to code. The opportunities it opens up.
